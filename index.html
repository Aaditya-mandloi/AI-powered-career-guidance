About The Project

CareerGuide AI is a final year B.Tech project designed to help students and professionals navigate their career paths effectively. Utilizing Natural Language Processing (NLP) and the power of IBM Watson Assistant, our intelligent system provides sophisticated intent recognition and entity extraction to understand your goals, skills, and preferences.

By conversational interaction, the Watson chatbot analyzes your inputs to suggest the most suitable tech roles, helping you bridge the gap between your current skills and your dream career.

Key Features

Personalized Career Suggestions

Receive tailored job role recommendations based on your unique background and aspirations.

Skill Recommendations

Identify skill gaps and discover exactly what you need to learn to achieve your career goals.

Learning Roadmaps

Follow structured, step-by-step guides from beginner to advanced levels for your chosen domain.

Real-Time Chatbot Support

Interact seamlessly with our Watson-powered AI agent for instant guidance anytime.
